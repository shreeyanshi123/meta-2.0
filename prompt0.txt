WHAT THE ENVIRONMENT DOES (mental model):

Each episode = one "tribunal round". The env dispatches a task to each of 4 worker agents
(Summariser, Ticket Resolver, Negotiator, Researcher). A Failure Injector secretly and
randomly corrupts 0–3 of those worker outputs with one of 4 failure types: HALLUCINATION,
COLLUSION, MANIPULATION, SILENCE. The Judge LLM (the agent being trained) observes all 4
outputs, then emits a structured verdict naming which workers misbehaved, what type, and
a plain-language explanation. The env scores the verdict against ground truth and returns
a rich, multi-dimensional reward. Training uses GRPO to update the Judge.
